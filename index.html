Social Media Content Strategy for Audience Engagement

Facebook

Target Audience: 25-54

  • Host live Q&A sessions to engage with your audience and provide insights.
  • Share in-depth articles, case studies, and industry reports.
  • Promote events, webinars, and workshops while interacting with followers.

Instagram

Target Audience: 18-34

  • Leverage Reels for quick tips, industry trends, and behind-the-scenes content.
  • Use Stories for polls, Q&A sessions, and interactive engagement.
  • Post carousels with educational and value-driven content.

X (Twitter)

Target Audience: 18-49

  • Post industry insights, trends, and real-time updates.
  • Engage in trending discussions and share expert opinions.
  • Run interactive polls to foster community engagement.

YouTube

Target Audience: 13-50+

  • Create long-form content such as tutorials, deep dives, and expert interviews.
  • Share vlogs and personal experiences to build relatability.
  • Upload webinar recordings and panel discussions for continued learning.

TikTok

Target Audience: 13-29

  • Create short-form, engaging videos with industry insights and trends.
  • Participate in challenges and leverage trending sounds for visibility.
  • Offer behind-the-scenes glimpses and relatable professional content.
